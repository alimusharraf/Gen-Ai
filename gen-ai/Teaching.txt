Teaching is the process of sharing knowledge, skills, and values with learners. 
Effective teaching involves clear communication, patience, and understanding the 
needs of students. Teachers use different methods such as lectures, discussions, 
practical activities, and technology to enhance learning. In modern education, 
digital tools and online platforms help teachers reach students beyond traditional 
classrooms. Good teaching not only improves academic performance but also develops 
critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving abilities in students.
